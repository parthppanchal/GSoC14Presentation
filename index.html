USER

The user, the one who will be assessed, will visit the link & enter the password to access the assessment.
Our UI is designed to help users to keep track of each question using a preview that displays marked for review, answered & unanswered questions. The marked for review questions overrides the status of being answered unless it's unmarked.
After submission, the result is available without skipping a second
